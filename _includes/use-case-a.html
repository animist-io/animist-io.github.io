Use case #1: Human Race

A mobile app for competitive running that pays

Two runners in New York want to bet each other $20 on a race across the Williamsburg Bridge. There's an app for that: Human Race. It helps them find two Animist enabled locations near either end of the bridge - a small bakery in LES and a natural foods store on Broadway in Brooklyn. The app can tap into these IOT nodes to authenticate the runners' presence and execute conditional payment contracts on the Ethereum chain.

They meet at the bakery and register the bet through their phones, transferring $20 from their digital wallets into an escrow contract. The app fires a starting shot. Whoever reaches the natural foods store first is detected by its Animist endpoint and automatically gets the proceeds of the wager.

Human Race also permits side bets on this race in jurisdictions where casual betting is legal. It sets aside a portion of that betting pool for a jackpot awarded to the race winner. Maybe the winner gets more than $20! Maybe not. Depends on their social media connections to people in England.
