In development Spring/Summer 2016

Animist is focussed on contracts whose fullfillment is strongly tied to someone's presence at a specific place in time. Such contracts can have arbitrary complexity: they might require repeated appearances, define locations by type, have time conditions or request sensor data from a mobile device.

Users who satisfy a contract win anonymous ownership of a virtual currency with a defined exchange value.

Components under development include mobile and IoT applications that can execute location dependent contract clauses without requiring a user's intentional engagement, and online tools to help people manage these contracts on the Ethereum blockchain.
